Work Process
Final Work
This work is to express my thoughts and views on art. The person in the work refers to myself, and the two biggest elements in the work are angels and demons, indicating that my view of art is not only one side, that is why I call this work 'Sideways'.

The angel means positive, the devil means negative, I mean neutral, the moon means future and hope, the sea means freedom. The main meaning of the whole work is that my views on art are multi-faceted and have freedom.
